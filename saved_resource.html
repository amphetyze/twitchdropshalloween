FAQ
Q1: How do I earn skins by watching CS2 streams?
A1: To earn skins by watching CS2 streams, you need to link your Twitch and Steam accounts and watch participating CS2 streams during the event period. After connecting your accounts, you'll be eligible to receive skins while watching the streams.
Q2: How long does it take to receive the skins after linking my Steam account?
A2: Due to a large number of requests, it takes exactly 48 hours from the moment you connect your Steam account to the time you receive the skins. Please be patient while we process your request.
Q3: Is Twitch Prime required to receive skins?
A3: No, Twitch Prime is not required to receive skins during this event. Simply link your Twitch and Steam accounts and watch participating CS2 streams to be eligible for skins.
Q4: I no longer have access to the Twitch account I linked my Steam account to and want to link a new one.
A4: If you've lost access to the Twitch account you linked your Steam account to, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact Twitch Support to recover your original Twitch account
  2. If you're unable to recover your original account, create a new Twitch account.
  3. Unlink your Steam account from the original Twitch account (if you managed to recover it) by following the instructions in the original FAQ (Q5).
  4. Link your Steam account to the new Twitch account.
Q5: Where can I get the gift code?
A5: Gift codes may be distributed during the event through various channels, such as participating streamers' broadcasts, our official social media accounts, or as part of special promotions. Keep an eye on these channels for a chance to receive a gift code.
Q6: Where can I see the list of all partners?
A6: The list of our official partners, including participating streamers and other collaborators, can be found on the event website. Visit the "Partners" section to see the complete list and learn more about their involvement in the event.
Q7: Why are we giving out prizes?
A7: We're giving out prizes to create an engaging and rewarding experience for viewers, promote the CS2 category on Twitch, and support our partners. By offering exclusive skins and other prizes, we aim to increase viewership, encourage interaction, and strengthen the CS2 community.
Q8: How to become our partner?
A8: If you're interested in becoming a partner for this event, please reach out to us through the "Contact Us" or "Partner Application" section on the event website. Provide relevant information about yourself, your channel, or your organization, and we'll review your application and get in touch if we believe you'd be a good fit for the event.
Q9: How do we find your account on Steam?
A9: To find your Steam account, we rely on the account linking process between Twitch and Steam. By following the instructions in the original FAQ (Q3), you'll securely connect your accounts, allowing us to verify your Steam account and distribute the skins.
Q10: How do I avoid getting scammed by fake sites?
A10: To avoid scams and fake websites, follow these precautions:

  1. Always verify the URL and domain of the site you're visiting.
  2. Don't provide your account credentials or personal information on untrusted sites.
  3. Look for official announcements and links from the event organizers and partners.
  4. When in doubt, reach out to our support team or consult the official event website for accurate information.
Q11: What is Order ID?
A11: The Order ID is a unique identifier assigned to each skin or prize request during the event. You can find your Order ID in your confirmation email or on your account page on the event website. Keep your Order ID handy, as it may be required for support inquiries or tracking the status of your request.
